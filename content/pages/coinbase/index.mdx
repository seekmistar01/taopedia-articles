# Coinbase

Coinbase is Bittensor's per-block mechanism for emission flow across subnets
([Coinbase Implementation](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/navigating-subtensor/emissions-coinbase),
[Emission](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/learn/emissions)).

## What It Represents

Each block, coinbase injects liquidity into subnet pools, accumulates pending emissions, and can
hand off to Yuma epoch execution at subnet boundaries
([Coinbase Implementation](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/navigating-subtensor/emissions-coinbase)).

The term names recurring emission processing, not a reward table, subnet strategy, or payout quote.
Halving steps change how much new TAO coinbase introduces over time as cumulative issuance crosses
documented thresholds
([Glossary: Halving](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/resources/glossary#halving),
[Halving Mechanisms](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/concepts/halving)).

Coinbase examples depend on which network you connect to. Per-block emission outcomes from one
environment should not be read as production behavior on another
([Bittensor Networks](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/concepts/bittensor-networks)).

## Distinction from Emission

Emission names the full creation-and-allocation process. Coinbase is the per-block engine inside
that flow ([Emission](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/learn/emissions),
[Coinbase Implementation](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/navigating-subtensor/emissions-coinbase)).

- **Emission** — injection and distribution across the protocol flow.
- **Coinbase** — per-block engine that advances that flow.

## Distinction from Block Reward

Block reward names the TAO amount minted in a block before distribution. Coinbase is the per-block
routine that mints and routes that emission
([Emission](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/learn/emissions),
[Coinbase Implementation](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/navigating-subtensor/emissions-coinbase)).

- **Coinbase** — the per-block process that mints and distributes emission.
- **Block reward** — the TAO amount minted in a block before distribution.

## Distinction from Tempo

Coinbase runs each block to accumulate pending emissions. Tempo is the recurring subnet interval
when accumulated alpha is distributed
([Glossary: Tempo](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/resources/glossary#tempo),
[Emission](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/learn/emissions)).

- **Coinbase** — per-block engine that accumulates pending emissions.
- **Tempo** — recurring interval when accumulated alpha is distributed.

## Distinction from Halving

Halving is the supply-triggered reduction that lowers the rate of new TAO entering emission flow.
Coinbase is the per-block process that advances that flow each block
([Glossary: Halving](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/resources/glossary#halving),
[Coinbase Implementation](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/navigating-subtensor/emissions-coinbase)).

- **Coinbase** — per-block emission engine that advances accounting each block.
- **Halving** — supply milestone that steps down the TAO issuance rate.

## Distinction from Issuance

Issuance names the broader supply flow of new TAO entering circulation. Coinbase advances emission
accounting each block toward that flow
([Glossary: Issuance](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/resources/glossary#issuance),
[Coinbase Implementation](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/navigating-subtensor/emissions-coinbase)).

- **Issuance** — broader supply flow of new TAO entering circulation.
- **Coinbase** — per-block engine that advances emission accounting.

## Distinction from Yuma Consensus

Coinbase accumulates pending emissions each block. Yuma Consensus converts validator weight
submissions into emission shares at tempo boundaries
([Coinbase Implementation](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/navigating-subtensor/emissions-coinbase),
[Yuma Consensus](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/learn/yuma-consensus)).

- **Coinbase** — per-block emission processing and pending-emission accumulation.
- **Yuma Consensus** — subnet consensus pass at epoch boundaries.

## Reader Boundary

Coinbase is the per-block Bittensor emission mechanism. It is not a payout quote, a subnet
performance statement, or a complete explanation of every reward outcome
([Coinbase Implementation](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/navigating-subtensor/emissions-coinbase),
[Emission](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/learn/emissions)).
