# Alpha Price

Alpha price is the TAO-denominated price of a subnet alpha token in Bittensor's Dynamic TAO pool
model. It comes from TAO reserve divided by alpha reserve in that subnet's paired pool
([Understanding Subnets](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/subnets/understanding-subnets),
[Emission](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/learn/emissions)).

## What It Represents

On netuid 1, documentation describes alpha price as TAO reserve divided by alpha reserve in that
subnet's pool. The quote uses alpha in reserve, not every unit of subnet alpha outstanding, and
names a reserve-derived TAO-per-alpha reading rather than a market-cap-style supply measure
([Understanding Subnets](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/subnets/understanding-subnets),
[Emission](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/learn/emissions)).

Examples tied to netuid 1 illustrate one subnet's reserve quote path. They should not be read as
representing production pool state on another network
([Bittensor Networks](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/concepts/bittensor-networks)).

Alpha price is an ideal reserve-based quote. Staking and unstaking can differ from the static ratio
because slippage accounts for reserve changes during a pool interaction
([Understanding Slippage](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/learn/slippage)).

## Distinction from Alpha Reserve

Alpha price is the TAO-per-alpha quote read from a subnet pool's reserves. Alpha reserve is the
pool-held alpha-side amount that pairs with the TAO reserve to set that quote
([Glossary: Alpha Price](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/resources/glossary#alpha-price),
[Glossary: Alpha Reserve](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/resources/glossary#alpha-reserve)).

- **Alpha price** — TAO-per-alpha quote from the reserve ratio.
- **Alpha reserve** — alpha-side pool inventory in that ratio.

Alpha reserve injection adds alpha in proportion to the subnet's current price so the pool can grow
without the injection itself shifting that reserve quote
([Emission: Alpha reserve injection](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/learn/emissions#alpha-reserve-injection)).
Cross-subnet TAO reserve injection is now flow-based, determined by net TAO inflows rather than
token prices
([Emission: Distribution across subnets](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/learn/emissions#distribution-across-subnets)).

## Distinction from Impermanent Loss

Alpha price is the reserve quote; impermanent loss is the value drift a liquidity provider can bear
as that quote moves away from where the provider entered
([User Liquidity Positions](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/liquidity-positions)).

- **Alpha price** — TAO-per-alpha quote read from pool reserves.
- **Impermanent loss** — drift cost as that quote moves.

## Distinction from Price Discovery

Price discovery is how trading and conversions settle a subnet's price through the pool. Alpha price
is the resulting TAO-denominated quote at a given moment
([Understanding Subnets](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/subnets/understanding-subnets),
[Slippage](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/learn/slippage)).

- **Price discovery** — process that settles price through trading.
- **Alpha price** — resulting TAO-denominated quote.

## Distinction from Dynamic TAO

Dynamic TAO is the tokenomics model with paired TAO and alpha reserves per subnet. Alpha price is
the observed TAO-per-alpha quote from one subnet pool in that model
([Glossary: Alpha Price](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/resources/glossary#alpha-price),
[Understanding Subnets](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/subnets/understanding-subnets)).

- **Dynamic TAO** — subnet tokenomics model with paired reserves.
- **Alpha price** — observed pool quote for alpha in context.

## Distinction from Yuma Consensus

Alpha price names a pool reserve quote. Yuma Consensus converts submitted validator weights into
emission shares after evaluation completes
([Glossary: Alpha Price](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/resources/glossary#alpha-price),
[Yuma Consensus](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/learn/yuma-consensus)).

- **Alpha price** — observed pool value for alpha in context.
- **Yuma Consensus** — on-chain weight aggregation that settles emissions.

## Distinction from Root Subnet

Subnet zero, also called the root subnet, does not maintain its own alpha currency or paired
TAO-and-alpha reserve pool like alpha subnets do
([Glossary: Root Subnet](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/resources/glossary#root-subnetsubnet-zero),
[Understanding Subnets](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/subnets/understanding-subnets)).

- **Alpha price** — TAO-per-alpha quote from an alpha subnet pool.
- **Root subnet** — TAO stake context without an alpha reserve pair.

Alpha price vocabulary applies to numbered alpha subnets, not to root staking comparisons.

Subnet deregistration still uses a smoothed alpha-price signal when selecting among eligible subnets
after the subnet limit is reached
([Subnet Deregistration](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/subnets/subnet-deregistration),
[Emission: De-registration remains price-based](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/learn/emissions#note-de-registration-remains-price-based)).

## Reader Boundary

Alpha price is a pool-derived tokenomics concept. It is not an operator instruction, an execution
amount, or a universal comparison across all subnets. Specific values belong to the network, subnet,
and reserve state being observed
([Understanding Subnets](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/subnets/understanding-subnets)).

## Distinction from TAO Reserve

Alpha price and the TAO reserve relate as a derived ratio and one of its inputs. The TAO reserve is
the TAO-side balance in a subnet's liquidity pool; alpha price is the pool price obtained from the
ratio of the TAO reserve to the alpha reserve, so a change in the TAO reserve moves the alpha price
rather than being the price itself
([Emission: TAO reserve injection](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/learn/emissions#tao-reserve-injection),
[Understanding Subnets](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/subnets/understanding-subnets)).

- **TAO reserve** — the TAO-side pool balance that forms the numerator of the price ratio.
- **Alpha price** — the pool price derived from the TAO-to-alpha reserve ratio.
