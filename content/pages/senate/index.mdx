# Senate

The Senate is the Bittensor governance body responsible for approving or disapproving proposals made
by the Triumvirate. The [Glossary](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/resources/glossary#senate)
describes it as a group of elected delegates that reviews Triumvirate proposals.

## Governance Role

The [Governance Overview](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/governance) places the Senate after the
Triumvirate in the proposal flow: the Triumvirate creates proposals, and the Senate approves or
rejects them. The [Senate](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/governance/senate) documentation provides
the Senate-side context for proposal review and voting.

## Reader Boundary

This article describes the Senate as a governance role. It does not list active senators, proposal
outcomes, or participation requirements. Readers should use official governance references for
current proposal records and detailed Senate context.
