# Proposal

A proposal is a Bittensor governance item put forward by the Triumvirate for Senate review. The
[Glossary](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/resources/glossary#proposal) defines it as a suggestion
or plan put forward by the Triumvirate for the Senate to vote on.

## Governance Context

The [Governance Overview](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/governance) describes the same separation
of roles: the Triumvirate creates proposals, and the Senate approves or rejects them. In Taopedia,
the useful boundary is that a proposal is the item being reviewed, while the Triumvirate and Senate
are the governance bodies around it.

## Reader Boundary

This article does not describe any live proposal, proposal record, or approval outcome. Readers
should use official governance references for current proposal details and Senate review context.
