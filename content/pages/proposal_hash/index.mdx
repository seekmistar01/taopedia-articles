# Proposal Hash

A proposal hash is the identifier used to refer to a proposal in Bittensor governance. The
[Proposal Hash glossary entry](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/resources/glossary#proposal-hash)
places the term in the Senate voting process, where a compact reference is needed for a proposal
under review.

## Governance Identifier

The proposal hash is an identifier, not the proposal content itself. In Bittensor governance,
proposals are the items reviewed by the Senate, while the hash gives readers and tooling a stable
way to point at the item. The [Governance Overview](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/governance)
gives the broader proposal-review frame.

## Proposal Relationship

A proposal and a proposal hash answer different questions. A
[proposal](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/resources/glossary#proposal) is the governance item put
forward for review, while the
[proposal hash](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/resources/glossary#proposal-hash) is the identifier
used to refer to that item during voting. Confusing the two can make an identifier sound like the
proposal body.

## Senate Review Context

The Senate uses proposal references during review and voting. The
[Senate documentation](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/governance/senate) places proposal review in
the governance body that approves or rejects Triumvirate proposals, so a proposal hash belongs to
that review workflow as a reference value rather than as an independent governance decision.

## Triumvirate Context

Proposal hashes sit downstream of proposal creation. The
[Governance Overview](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/governance) separates creation by the
Triumvirate from Senate approval, and the hash is useful because the same proposal must be referred
to across that review path. The term does not rename either governance body.

## Reading the Term

Proposal hash should be read as governance reference vocabulary. It does not list active proposals,
predict outcomes, or explain the substance of a proposed change. The
[Proposal Hash glossary entry](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/resources/glossary#proposal-hash)
anchors the identifier meaning, while governance and Senate references provide the surrounding
process context.
