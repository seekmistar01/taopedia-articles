# Existential Deposit

Existential deposit is the minimum TAO balance threshold for a Bittensor account to remain
represented. The
[Existential Deposit glossary entry](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/resources/glossary#existential-deposit)
places the term in account-balance vocabulary rather than staking, fee, or subnet scoring
vocabulary.

## Balance Threshold

The concept is a threshold, not a reward category. The
[Existential Deposit glossary entry](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/resources/glossary#existential-deposit)
ties the term to the minimum amount of TAO required for an account to exist. That makes the term
about account retention rather than miner evaluation or validator influence.

## TAO Context

The threshold is expressed in TAO. The
[TAO glossary entry](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/resources/glossary#tao) gives the token
context, while existential deposit narrows the question to the minimum account-balance boundary. The
two terms are related because the threshold is measured in the network token.

## Dust-Account Boundary

Existential deposit helps separate meaningful account records from dust-account buildup. The
[Existential Deposit glossary entry](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/resources/glossary#existential-deposit)
connects balances below the threshold with account removal to conserve resources, so the concept is
best read as a resource-boundary term.

## Difference from Fees

Existential deposit is not the same as a transaction fee. Fees relate to the cost of an action,
while existential deposit is a retained-balance threshold. The
[Transaction Fees](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/learn/fees) reference provides fee context, which
keeps the two account-balance ideas separate.

## Difference from Staking

Existential deposit is also different from staking.
[Staking and delegation](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/staking-and-delegation/delegation) concerns
TAO placed behind validator support, while existential deposit concerns the minimum account balance
boundary. A staking amount and an account-retention threshold answer different questions.

## Reading the Term

Existential deposit should be read as balance-threshold vocabulary. It does not name a live account
value, staking recommendation, subnet metric, or recovery process. The
[Existential Deposit glossary entry](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/resources/glossary#existential-deposit)
anchors the term, with TAO, fee, and staking references supplying nearby context.
