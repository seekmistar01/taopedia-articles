Quantum Compute is Bittensor Subnet 48. Its on-chain identity describes the subnet as Quantum
Computing, lists [qbittensorlabs.com](https://www.qbittensorlabs.com/) as the subnet URL, and lists
[qbittensor-labs/quantum-compute](https://github.com/qbittensor-labs/quantum-compute) as the
subnet's GitHub repository.

## What Quantum Compute Provides

The Quantum Compute README describes a Bittensor subnet for making quantum-computing resources
available through a decentralized network. Its public documentation focuses on access to gate-model
quantum computers that can execute OpenQASM 2.0 or 3.0 circuits.

At the subnet level, Quantum Compute turns quantum circuit execution into a miner-scoring and
service-delivery workflow. Validators receive circuit jobs, distribute them to available miners,
check returned results, and publish weights through [[Yuma Consensus]].

## Miner and Validator Roles

Miners provide quantum compute capacity. The miner documentation says miners connect to real
gate-model quantum computers, receive circuit jobs from validators, execute them on a target device,
and return result data such as counts, bitstrings, metadata, and timestamps.

Validators coordinate job distribution and quality assurance. The validator documentation describes
validators as tracking active miners, sending work to suitable miners, checking completed responses,
handling failed jobs, and returning successful results to the job server. Operators should treat the
repository as the source of truth for current participant guidance.

## On-Chain Identity

Live SN48 data is available on [TaoStats](https://taostats.io/subnets/48). Readers can also
reproduce the chain identity fields with the Bittensor CLI command documented in the
[btcli reference](https://docs.learnbittensor.org/btcli/#btcli-subnets-get-identity):
`btcli subnets get-identity --netuid 48 --network finney`.

That Finney identity reports the subnet name as Quantum Compute, the subnet URL as
qbittensorlabs.com, and the GitHub repo as qbittensor-labs/quantum-compute.
